Tigers are the largest of all cat species and easily recognizable by their
iconic stripes.  Known as Apex predators, these cats are Powerful and 
efficient hunters with an anatomy designed for eating meat.  Tigers play
an important roll in natural population control, but are being forced out
of their natural habitats by human encroachment and extermination.  
Despite human fears, Tigers have a long history with humanity.  They
appear on many flags, coats of arms, as modern mascot and in the legends
down through the histories of many cultures around the world.  Facing
possible extinction, the plight of Tigers today as a species is grim.
