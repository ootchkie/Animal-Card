Tigris, Panthera

Tigers can stay up to two weeks without food, but when they catch prey, they can eat 75 pounds of meat at once!

  • Tigers are the largest of all cat species and easily recognizable.
  • They are on many flags, coats of arms, and in legends down through history.
  • They are frequently and famously portrayed in many well known movies and films.
  • Known as Apex predators, Tigers play an important roll in natural population control.
  • Only six subspecies survive Today due to a number of factors including loss of habitat and overhunting.
  • Facing extinction from human pressures, numbers are dramatacly low between 3,062 and 3,948 individuals.

These cats are powerful and efficient predators with an anatomy designed for hunting.
