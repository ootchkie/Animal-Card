Tigris, Panthera

Tigers can stay up to two weeks without food, but when they catch prey, they can eat 75 pounds of meat at once!

  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Phylum: Chordata
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Order: Carnivora
  • Suborder: Feliformia
  • Family: Felidae
  • Genus: Panthera
  • Species: P.Tigris
  • Status: Endangered

Tigers are the largest of all cat species and easily recognizable by their iconic stripes. Known as Apex predators, these cats are Powerful and efficient hunters with an anatomy designed for eating meat. Tigers play an important roll in natural population control, but are being forced out of their natural habitats by human encroachment and extermination. Despite human fears, Tigers have a long history with humanity. They appear on many flags, coats of arms, as modern mascot and in the legends and histories of many cultures around the world. They are frequently and famously portrayed in many well known movies and films Facing possible extinction, the plight of Tigers today as a species is grim. Only 6 subspecies of the animal are left and their numbers in the wild are reduced to a low number, estimates are for appx. 3,500 total. That is bad.
